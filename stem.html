What are the career options available for students who pursue the STEM strand in the Philippines?

This question is often asked by students who want to know the potential career paths they can pursue after completing the STEM strand. Common career options include engineering, computer science, architecture, medicine, research, data analysis, and more.

What are the subjects included in the STEM strand?

The STEM strand typically includes subjects such as Mathematics, General Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Earth and Life Science, Statistics and Probability, and Research.

Can students from the STEM strand pursue non-STEM careers?

Yes, students from the STEM strand are not limited to pursuing only STEM careers. The skills and knowledge gained in the STEM strand can be applied to various fields, including business, management, finance, and even the arts. The STEM strand provides a strong foundation that can be beneficial in a wide range of career paths.
